About Us

Ramciel Techcom South Sudan, was established by South Sudanese Students in various Indian Universities in 2019. It is designed to be a premier society of Information Technology and related areas. The society will be converted into a multipurpose company when there is enough capital to facilitate the requirements needed.

We offers trainings in Computer related courses, basic Engineering, and web development. The Web Development is offered in the most accurate and understandable way, we also offers other personal developmental courses such as project planning and business management
